Privacy Policy

Last reviewed: July 19, 2026

Current status: Creator Revenue Calculator has not been approved by Google AdSense. Advertising scripts and Microsoft Clarity are not loaded. Google Analytics is optional and remains blocked until you explicitly allow it.

Calculator inputs

The calculators run in your browser. Values you enter are used to calculate results on your device and are not intentionally transmitted to Creator Revenue Calculator or stored in our database. We do not offer user accounts, and the site does not currently operate an email signup or results-delivery form.

Hosting and routine request data

The site is hosted by Vercel. Like ordinary web hosting, Vercel may process request information such as an IP address, requested URL, browser information, timestamps, and security or diagnostic data to deliver and protect the site. We do not use this information to build advertising profiles. Hosting-log availability and retention depend on Vercel's current systems, plan, and policies, so we do not promise a fixed deletion schedule.

Advertising and analytics

Google AdSense and Microsoft Clarity are not enabled. Google Analytics loads only after you select "Allow analytics." If allowed, it receives only the current page title and path after the URL query string is removed. Calculator inputs, calculator results, email addresses, and other form values are not sent. Advertising storage, ad personalization, Google Signals, and enhanced measurement are disabled. Without permission, the Google Analytics script is not downloaded.

The site does not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. An AdSense publisher declaration may remain in ads.txt for ownership verification; it does not itself load ads or tracking.

External links

The icons and chart software used by the calculators are hosted on Creator Revenue Calculator and do not require requests to a third-party content delivery network. Links to creator platforms, source materials, affiliate products, or other websites take you to services governed by their own privacy policies.

Affiliate links

Some product links may be Amazon Associates links. If you follow one, Amazon receives the request and may associate purchases with our referral tag. We may earn a commission without increasing your price. Amazon's handling of information is governed by its privacy notice. Affiliate relationships do not influence calculator results.

Local browser storage

The theme control may save a light or dark appearance preference in your browser's local storage. The analytics control stores whether you allowed or denied analytics so the choice can be honored on later visits. These preferences stay on your device and can be removed through browser settings. Use the persistent Privacy choices button to change analytics permission. See the Cookie Notice.

Children

The service is a general-audience estimation tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly request personal information from children.

Your choices and questions

You can clear local site data and choose not to follow external or affiliate links. For privacy questions, email privacy@creatorrevenuecalculator.com.

Changes

We may revise this policy when actual data practices change. The review date will be updated after a substantive review.
