# TikTok Calculator Data Sources (2026)

## TikTok Creator Fund Rates (2026)
- **Base rate**: $0.02-$0.04 per 1,000 views
- **Factors affecting rate**: Video engagement, watch time, region, content category
- **Payment threshold**: $50 minimum payout
- Source: TikTok Creator Fund documentation, 2026 update

## TikTok LIVE Gifts
- **Gift value to creator**: Approximately 50% of gift value
- **Diamond conversion**: 1 diamond = $0.05 to creator (after TikTok's cut)
- **Typical earnings**: $50-$500 per LIVE for mid-sized creators
- Source: TikTok LIVE monetization terms, 2026

## TikTok Creator Marketplace (Brand Deals)
Based on 2026 industry reports:

### By Follower Tier:
- **10K-50K followers**: $100-$500 per post
- **50K-100K followers**: $500-$1,500 per post  
- **100K-500K followers**: $1,500-$5,000 per post
- **500K-1M followers**: $5,000-$15,000 per post
- **1M+ followers**: $15,000-$50,000+ per post

### By Content Type:
- **Feed posts**: Base rates shown above
- **LIVE brand integration**: 2-3x feed post rates
- **Series/ongoing partnerships**: 3-5x single post rates
- **Hashtag challenges**: $10,000-$100,000+ campaigns

Source: Influencer Marketing Hub 2026 Report, TikTok Creator Marketplace data

## TikTok Pulse (Premium Ad Revenue Share)
- **Top 4% of videos** qualify for Pulse program
- **Revenue share**: 50% of ad revenue on qualifying videos
- **Requirements**: 100K+ followers, consistent content, community guidelines compliance
- Source: TikTok Pulse program terms, 2026

## Important Notes
- **US/UK/Canada rates** are 2-3x higher than other regions
- **Engagement rate** significantly impacts earnings (3%+ ideal)
- **Video length**: Longer videos (1min+) earn more than short videos
- **Music usage**: Original audio performs better than trending sounds for monetization
- **Seasonal variation**: Q4 earnings typically 30-50% higher

## Data Collection Methodology
- Aggregated from TikTok's public documentation
- Cross-referenced with creator self-reported earnings
- Verified against influencer marketing agency data
- Adjusted for 2026 market conditions
