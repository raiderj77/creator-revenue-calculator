## Affiliate vs CPM Comparison (2026)
Based on 2026 digital advertising data:

### When Affiliate Performs Better:
- **High-ticket items** ($100+ average order value)
- **Digital products** (30-50% commissions)
- **Recurring subscriptions** (stable long-term income)
- **Niche products** (targeted audience, higher conversion)

### When CPM Performs Better:
- **Low-ticket items** (under $30 average order value)
- **Mass audience content** (entertainment, news)
- **Brand awareness campaigns** (not direct response)
- **Young demographics** (lower purchasing power)

## Important Notes
- **Cookie duration**: Typically 30-90 days for most programs
- **Payment thresholds**: $50-$100 minimum payout common
- **Payment schedules**: Net-30 to Net-60 standard
- **Geographic variations**: US/UK/Canada rates 2-3x higher than other regions
- **Seasonal variations**: Q4 earnings typically 30-50% higher
