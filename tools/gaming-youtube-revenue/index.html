Gaming YouTube CPM Data 2026

Gaming content has one of the lowest CPM rates on YouTube, but successful creators diversify their income.

Gaming CPM by Content Type

  • Gameplay/Let's Play: $0.50-$1.50
  • Esports Coverage: $1.00-$2.00
  • Game Reviews: $1.50-$2.50
  • Game Tutorials: $1.80-$2.50
  • Gaming News: $2.00-$2.50
  • PC Hardware: $2.00-$3.00

Gaming CPM by Audience Location

  • United States: $1.20-$2.50
  • United Kingdom: $1.00-$2.00
  • Canada/Australia: $0.80-$1.80
  • Western Europe: $0.60-$1.50
  • Asia (excl. Japan): $0.20-$0.80
  • Latin America: $0.10-$0.50

Why Gaming CPM is Lower

  • Younger Audience: 13-24 year olds have lower purchasing power
  • High Competition: Millions of gaming creators
  • Copyright Issues: Game footage/music limits monetization
  • Advertiser Perception: Gaming seen as "entertainment" not "premium"
  • Ad Block Usage: Gamers use ad blockers at higher rates

How Gaming YouTubers Really Make Money

Successful gaming creators earn most of their income from sources beyond YouTube ads:

Sponsorships & Brand Deals

Earnings: $500-$10,000+ per video

Game publishers, gaming chairs, PC parts brands, energy drinks. Top gaming creators earn 2-10x more from sponsorships than YouTube ads.

Affiliate Marketing

Earnings: 5-15% commission

Amazon Associates (gaming gear), Elgato, NZXT, Secret Lab chairs. Gaming creators earn $50-$500+ monthly from affiliate links in video descriptions.

Merchandise

Earnings: $1,000-$50,000+ monthly

T-shirts, hoodies, gaming accessories with channel branding. Successful gaming merch stores can exceed YouTube ad revenue.

Patreon/Memberships

Earnings: $1,000-$20,000+ monthly

Exclusive content, early access, Discord perks. Gaming communities are highly engaged and willing to support creators directly.

Twitch Streaming

Earnings: $500-$50,000+ monthly

Subscriptions, bits, donations. Most successful gaming YouTubers also stream on Twitch for additional revenue.

Courses & Coaching

Earnings: $100-$5,000 per student

Game-specific tutorials, streaming setup courses, content creation coaching. Educational gaming content has higher perceived value.

How to Increase Gaming YouTube Earnings

1. Diversify Content Types

Mix gameplay with higher-CPM content: game tutorials, industry analysis, hardware reviews. Educational gaming content earns 2-3x higher CPM than pure gameplay.

2. Target Older Audiences

Create content for 25+ gamers: retro gaming, game development, industry news. Older audiences have higher disposable income and attract better advertisers.

3. Build a Community

Engage viewers through Discord, Patreon, memberships. Community support often exceeds ad revenue for mid-sized gaming channels.

4. Optimize for Search

Create "how to" guides, game walkthroughs, tutorial content. Search-driven gaming content has longer shelf life and attracts consistent views.

5. Cross-Promote Revenue Streams

Mention sponsors, affiliate links, merchandise in every video. Successful gaming creators make it easy for fans to support them beyond watching ads.

6. Analyze Your Analytics

Check which videos earn highest CPM, which audience locations pay most. Double down on content that performs well financially, not just view-wise.
