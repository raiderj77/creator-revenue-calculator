# Twitch Calculator Data Sources (2026)

## Twitch Revenue Splits (2026)

### Subscriptions
- **Affiliates & Standard Partners**: 50/50 split (50% to streamer, 50% to Twitch)
- **Premium Partners**: Negotiable splits (typically 60/40 or 70/30 for top streamers)
- **Tier 1**: $4.99 → $2.50 to streamer
- **Tier 2**: $9.99 → $5.00 to streamer  
- **Tier 3**: $24.99 → $12.50 to streamer
- Source: Twitch Partner & Affiliate Agreement, 2026

### Bits
- **1 Bit = $0.01** (gross value)
- **Streamer receives**: ~$0.80 per 100 bits (after Twitch's cut)
- **Conversion**: 100 bits = $1.00 gross, $0.80 net to streamer
- Source: Twitch Bits terms, 2026

### Ad Revenue
- **Standard split**: 55/45 (55% to streamer, 45% to Twitch)
- **CPM rates by category**: $3-$12 per 1,000 ad impressions
- **Ad density**: 3-8 minutes of ads per hour recommended
- Source: Twitch Ad Revenue program, 2026

## Twitch Partner Requirements (2026)
- **Path to Partner**: 75+ average concurrent viewers over 30 days
- **Streaming requirements**: 12+ unique streaming days, 25+ hours streamed
- **Content guidelines**: Follow Community Guidelines, Terms of Service
- **Application review**: Not automatic, manual review process
- Source: Twitch Partner Program requirements, 2026

## Sponsorship Rates (2026)
Based on average viewer count:
- **50-100 viewers**: $50-$200 per integration
- **100-500 viewers**: $200-$1,000 per integration  
- **500-1,000 viewers**: $1,000-$2,500 per integration
- **1,000-5,000 viewers**: $2,500-$10,000 per integration
- **5,000-10,000 viewers**: $10,000-$25,000 per integration
- **10,000+ viewers**: $25,000-$100,000+ per integration

## Important Notes
- **US/Canada audiences** earn 2-3x more than other regions
- **Prime Time streaming** (7-11 PM local) earns 20-40% more
- **Engagement matters**: Higher chat activity increases CPM
- **Ad density**: More frequent ads increase revenue but may reduce viewership
- **Platform fees**: Additional 5-10% for payment processing in some regions

## Data Collection Methodology
- Aggregated from Twitch's public documentation

- Verified against streaming analytics platforms
