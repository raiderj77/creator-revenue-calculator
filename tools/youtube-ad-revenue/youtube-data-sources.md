# YouTube Calculator Data Sources (2026)

## Revenue Split
- **55/45 split**: 55% to creator, 45% to YouTube (YouTube Partner Program Terms, 2026)
- Source: YouTube Partner Program documentation, 2026 update

## YouTube Partner Program Requirements
- **Basic monetization**: 500 subscribers + 3,000 watch hours (last 365 days)
- **Full monetization**: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (last 365 days)
- Source: YouTube Creator Academy, 2026 requirements

## Important Notes
- CPM = Cost Per Mille (per 1,000 views)
- Rates shown are gross CPM before YouTube's 45% fee
- US/Canada audiences earn 2-3x higher CPM than other regions
- Rates vary by season (Q4 typically 20-40% higher)
- Shorts CPM typically 30-50% of long-form video CPM
