Data Sources & Methodology

Transparent information about where our 2026 YouTube earnings data comes from

YouTube Partner Program Terms

Revenue Split: 55/45 (55% to creator, 45% to YouTube)

YPP Requirements: 500 subscribers + 3,000 watch hours (basic), 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (full)

Source: YouTube Creator Academy & Partner Program documentation, 2026 updates

CPM Rate Data

Primary Sources: TubeBuddy Industry Report 2026, Social Blade Analytics 2026, VidIQ State of YouTube 2026

Methodology: Aggregated data from multiple industry reports, cross-referenced with creator surveys

Geographic Adjustment: US/Canada rates adjusted 2-3x higher than global averages

Industry Benchmarks

Niche CPM Ranges: Based on 2026 creator economy reports across 10 major categories

Seasonal Adjustments: Q4 rates adjusted +30-50% based on historical advertising patterns

Validation: Cross-checked against self-reported creator earnings on platforms like Twitter and Reddit

Disclaimer: This calculator provides estimates based on 2026 industry averages. Actual earnings may vary based on content quality, audience demographics, engagement rates, and market conditions. All data is sourced from publicly available industry reports and platform documentation.
