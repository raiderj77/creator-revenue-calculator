3

Join Ad Networks & Platforms

Start earning through established networks:

  • Apply to networks: Spotify, Apple, Megaphone, Acast
  • Understand fees: Most take 20-30% of revenue
  • Set ad rates: Based on downloads and niche
  • Choose ad formats: Pre-roll, mid-roll, post-roll
  • Track performance: Use network analytics

Tip: Start with smaller networks (Podcorn, AdvertiseCast) before applying to premium networks.

4

Secure Direct Sponsorships

Move beyond networks for higher earnings:

  • Build media kit: Stats, demographics, past sponsors
  • Reach out to brands: Those aligned with your niche
  • Offer host-read ads: Command 30-50% premium
  • Create packages: Episode sponsorships, series sponsors
  • Track results: Provide brands with performance data

Tip: Direct sponsorships typically pay 20-40% more than network rates.

5

Diversify Revenue Streams

Build sustainable podcast business:

  • Affiliate marketing: 10-30% commissions on products
  • Premium content: Bonus episodes, early access
  • Merchandise: Branded products for superfans
  • Live events: Meetups, conferences, workshops
  • Consulting/services: Leverage podcast authority

Tip: The most successful podcasters have 3+ revenue streams.
