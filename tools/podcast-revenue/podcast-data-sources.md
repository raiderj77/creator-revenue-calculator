# Podcast Calculator Data Sources (2026)

## IAB Podcast CPM Standards (2026)
Based on Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) 2026 standards:

### Ad Placement CPM Rates:
- **Pre-roll ads (15-30 sec)**: $18-$25 CPM
- **Mid-roll ads (60-90 sec)**: $25-$35 CPM  
- **Post-roll ads (15-30 sec)**: $15-$22 CPM
- **Host-read ads**: 30-50% premium over produced ads
- **Dynamic ad insertion**: Standard CPM rates apply

### IAB Measurement Standards:
- **Download definition**: Episode file partially downloaded (IAB V2.1)
- **Measurement window**: 30 days standard for podcast advertising
- **Listener verification**: IP address, user agent, download completion tracking
- Source: IAB Podcast Technical Guidelines 2026

## Podcast Network vs Direct Sponsorship
- **Ad networks**: Take 20-30% of revenue, provide ad sales and insertion
- **Direct sponsorships**: Higher rates (20-40% premium), lower fees (10-15%)
- **Hybrid models**: Some networks offer 70/30 splits for top shows
- **Minimum requirements**: Typically 5,000+ downloads per episode for networks

## Important Notes
- **US/Canada audiences** earn 2-3x more than other regions
- **Download numbers** are typically measured over 30 days
- **Listener loyalty** (completion rate) affects CPM rates
- **Production quality** significantly impacts sponsorship rates
- **Niche specificity** often commands higher CPM than broad topics
