How Beehiiv Monetizes Newsletters

Beehiiv provides creators with three primary monetization avenues, structured to maximize revenue as a newsletter grows. First, and most significant, is subscription revenue. On Beehiiv’s “Scale” plan—the tier required for monetization—the platform charges a 0% fee on subscription earnings. This is a direct contrast to competitors like Substack, which take a 10% cut. Writers keep 100% of their subscription income, paying only the payment processor fee (approximately 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction). Second is the Boosts network, an internal advertising system where writers can recommend other newsletters to their audience and earn a bounty for each new subscriber they refer. Third, Beehiiv offers a built-in ad network for larger publications, allowing them to sell display and sponsored placement slots directly. The platform cost itself ranges from $49 to $99 per month for the necessary plans, an expense easily offset by even modest subscription success.

Beehiiv Boosts — The Unique Monetization Feature

Boosts represent Beehiiv’s most innovative income stream. It functions as a peer-to-peer recommendation network. A writer can “boost” another newsletter they genuinely admire to their own subscriber list via a dedicated section in their email or website. For every new, verified subscriber that joins the boosted newsletter via their link, the referring creator earns a bounty, typically between $1 and $3. This creates a powerful, organic growth mechanism where established newsletters have a financial incentive to help newer ones, and writers can generate meaningful revenue simply by curating quality content for their audience. A newsletter with 10,000 subscribers might earn an extra $300-$1,000 per month from Boosts alone, depending on their audience’s responsiveness and how strategically they participate in the network.

Realistic Monthly Income for Beehiiv Creators

Setting realistic expectations is crucial. In 2026, the majority of Beehiiv writers who have actively monetized for at least one year and have built an audience of 2,000-10,000 subscribers report monthly incomes between $500 and $5,000. This income is typically a hybrid of a core group of paying subscribers (often 200-800 people) and consistent Boosts revenue. Very few creators achieve “overnight” success; income builds gradually as trust and audience value deepen. A writer in a specialized field like “AI for Biologists” may reach $5,000 per month with only 3,000 subscribers due to high-value content, while a “general interest” culture writer might need 15,000 subscribers to achieve the same. The path to sustainable income almost always involves a consistent publishing schedule, audience engagement, and strategic use of Boosts.

Growing a Beehiiv Newsletter to $1K/Month

Reaching the $1,000 per month milestone is a key goal that validates a newsletter as a serious venture. To achieve this on Beehiiv, a creator typically needs a combined strategy. First, build a free subscriber base to at least 5,000 through consistent, valuable content and promotion on social media or other channels. Second, launch a paid subscription offering with multiple tiers (e.g., $5/month for archives, $15/month for a community). A conversion rate of 4-5% from the free list would yield 200-250 paying subscribers, generating the core $1,000. Third, actively participate in the Boosts network. Recommending two newsletters per month to an engaged list of 5,000 could generate an additional $100-$400, accelerating the path to $1k. This target is often attainable within 12-18 months of dedicated, strategic work.

FAQ

How quickly can I start earning money on Beehiiv?

You can technically enable payments as soon as you launch your paid tiers on the Scale plan. However, earning meaningful income requires an audience. Most creators focus on building a free subscriber base for 6-12 months before launching paid subscriptions. Some revenue from Boosts can occur earlier if you actively participate in the network, but substantial income is contingent on having a sizable, engaged readership to convert or refer.

Is Beehiiv better for making money than Substack?

For creators focused purely on maximizing subscription revenue, Beehiiv’s 0% fee model is mathematically superior to Substack’s 10% cut. For a creator earning $10,000 per month, that’s an extra $1,000 retained with Beehiiv. The Boosts network also provides a unique, additional income stream Substack lacks. However, Substack may offer better initial discovery within its app for certain topics. The “better” platform depends on whether you prioritize keeping all your subscription revenue and accessing Boosts (Beehiiv) or potentially benefiting from Substack’s established reader ecosystem.

What are the costs of using Beehiiv?

Beehiiv operates on a tiered subscription model for creators. The free “Launch” plan is very limited. The “Grow” plan starts at $49/month and includes essential features but does not allow monetization. The “Scale” plan, required for paid subscriptions and Boosts, starts at $99/month. This monthly fee is your primary cost, as Beehiiv charges 0% on your earnings. You also pay standard Stripe or PayPal payment processing fees on each transaction (around 2.9% + $0.30).
