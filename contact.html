How to Report a Bug

If you have found a bug or issue with one of our calculators, please include the following information in your email to help us resolve it quickly:

  • Calculator name: Which tool were you using? (e.g., YouTube Revenue Calculator)
  • What happened: Describe the unexpected behavior you observed
  • What you expected: Describe what you thought should happen
  • Steps to reproduce: What inputs or actions led to the issue?
  • Browser and device: e.g., Chrome 120 on Windows 11, Safari on iPhone 15
  • Screenshots: If possible, attach a screenshot showing the problem

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators free to use?

Yes, all of our calculators are completely free with no account required. We support our site through non-intrusive advertisements.

How accurate are the revenue estimates?

Our estimates are based on industry data and updated 2026 rates, but actual earnings vary significantly based on niche, audience, geography, and other factors. Use our tools as a starting point for understanding potential earnings, not as guaranteed income projections.

Do you store my calculator inputs?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit the data you enter into our tools.

Can I use your estimates in my content or presentations?

You are welcome to reference our estimates with proper attribution and a link back to creatorrevenuecalculator.com. Please make it clear that the figures are estimates and not guaranteed earnings.
