Common Questions

Calculator data seems wrong?

Our calculators use 2026 industry averages. Actual earnings vary significantly based on niche, audience demographics, content quality, and platform algorithm changes. If you believe a specific data point is outdated, please email us with the correction and source.

Want your platform added?

We're always looking to expand our tool suite. If there's a platform or revenue stream we don't cover yet, let us know.

Partnership or advertising inquiries?

For business inquiries, please email us directly at hello@creatorrevenuecalculator.com with "Partnership" in the subject line.

Response Time

We typically respond to emails within 1-2 business days. For bug reports submitted via GitHub, we aim to triage within 48 hours.
