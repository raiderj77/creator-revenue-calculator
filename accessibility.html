Accessibility Statement

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Our Commitment

Creator Revenue Calculator is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We believe that every content creator, regardless of disability, should be able to access and use our revenue estimation tools. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are continually working to improve the experience for all users. We regularly review and test our site to identify and address accessibility barriers.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features across our website:

Keyboard Navigation

  • All interactive elements (links, buttons, form inputs, sliders) are accessible via keyboard
  • A logical tab order is maintained throughout each page
  • Focus indicators are visible on all interactive elements when navigating with a keyboard
  • Calculator forms can be fully operated using keyboard controls alone

Screen Reader Compatibility

  • Semantic HTML elements are used to provide meaningful page structure
  • Form labels are properly associated with their input fields
  • Images and icons include appropriate alt text or aria-labels
  • Calculator results are announced to screen readers when calculations are performed
  • Headings are used in a logical hierarchy to aid navigation

Visual Design

  • Color contrast ratios meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 Level AA requirements (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
  • Information is not conveyed through color alone; additional visual cues such as icons and text labels are provided
  • Text is resizable up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
  • The layout is responsive and adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels

Content and Readability

  • Page language is declared in the HTML lang attribute
  • Content is written in clear, plain language
  • Link text is descriptive and meaningful (avoiding generic "click here" phrases)
  • Error messages in calculator forms are clear and provide guidance on how to correct input

Motion and Animation

  • Animations are minimal and do not include flashing or strobing effects
  • Users who have enabled "prefers-reduced-motion" in their operating system will experience reduced or no animations
  • No content auto-plays or auto-scrolls without user control

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Our current conformance status is as follows:

  • Perceivable: We provide text alternatives for non-text content, ensure sufficient color contrast, and make content adaptable to different presentations.
  • Operable: All functionality is available from a keyboard, users have adequate time to interact with content, and navigation is consistent and predictable.
  • Understandable: Text is readable and predictable, and input assistance is provided where needed.
  • Robust: Content is compatible with current and future assistive technologies, and we use valid, semantic HTML.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we are aware of the following areas where improvements are ongoing:

  • Third-Party Advertisements: Ads served through Google AdSense are outside our direct control and may not meet all accessibility standards. We encourage Google to improve the accessibility of their ad content.
  • Older Browser Support: Some accessibility features may not work optimally in older browsers. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website has been tested and is compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • NVDA (NonVisual Desktop Access) with Firefox and Chrome
  • VoiceOver with Safari on macOS and iOS
  • TalkBack with Chrome on Android
  • JAWS (Job Access With Speech) with Chrome and Edge
  • Keyboard-only navigation in all major browsers

Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Creator Revenue Calculator. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, have difficulty using any part of our site, or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. We take all accessibility feedback seriously and will work to address issues promptly.

You can reach us at:

  • Email: hello@creatorrevenuecalculator.com
  • Subject line: Accessibility Feedback

When reporting an accessibility issue, please include:

  • A description of the problem you encountered
  • The page URL where the issue occurred
  • The browser and assistive technology you were using
  • Any screenshots or other details that can help us understand and reproduce the issue

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and to resolve identified issues as quickly as possible.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility concern, you may file a complaint with your local accessibility enforcement authority or the relevant government body in your jurisdiction.
