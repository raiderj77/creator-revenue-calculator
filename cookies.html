Cookie Notice

Last reviewed: July 13, 2026

Creator Revenue Calculator does not intentionally set advertising or analytics cookies. Google AdSense, Google Analytics, and Microsoft Clarity are disabled.

Local storage

The theme control may store a light or dark appearance preference in local storage. Local storage is browser data but is not a cookie and is not sent with every web request. You can remove it through browser site-data controls.

Hosting technology

Vercel hosts and protects the site. Its infrastructure may use essential technical mechanisms or process routine request data to deliver pages, balance traffic, and prevent abuse. We do not use those mechanisms for behavioral advertising.

External links

The calculator interface does not load icon or chart code from third-party content delivery networks. Following an external or affiliate link creates a normal request to that destination, which may use cookies or storage under its own policy.

If our practices change

We will update this notice and add an appropriate consent mechanism before enabling optional analytics or advertising cookies where consent is required. Because no optional cookies are currently set by us, the site does not display a cookie-consent banner.
