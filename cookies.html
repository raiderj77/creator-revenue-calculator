What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and supply information to website owners. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device until they expire or you delete them) or "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser).

How We Use Cookies

Creator Revenue Calculator uses cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic, display relevant advertisements, and remember your preferences. We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information. Our calculators process all data locally in your browser and do not store any of your inputs.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features such as remembering your cookie consent preference. Without these cookies, certain parts of the site cannot function.

  • cookie_consent - Stores your cookie consent choice. Duration: persistent (localStorage). Set by: Creator Revenue Calculator.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information about which pages you visit, how long you stay, and how you arrived at our site. All data is aggregated and anonymized.

  • _ga - Used to distinguish unique users. Duration: 2 years. Set by: Google Analytics.
  • _ga_* - Used to maintain session state. Duration: 2 years. Set by: Google Analytics.
  • _gid - Used to distinguish unique users. Duration: 24 hours. Set by: Google Analytics.
  • _gat - Used to throttle request rate. Duration: 1 minute. Set by: Google Analytics.

Advertising Cookies

We display advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free operation of our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. These cookies enable Google and its partners to show you relevant advertisements.

  • NID - Used by Google to store preferences and personalize ads. Duration: 6 months. Set by: Google.
  • IDE - Used by Google DoubleClick to register and report user actions after viewing or clicking an ad. Duration: 1 year. Set by: Google.
  • DSID - Used to identify a signed-in user on non-Google sites for ad personalization. Duration: 2 weeks. Set by: Google.
  • __gads - Used to measure ad interactions and prevent the same ads from being shown repeatedly. Duration: 13 months. Set by: Google AdSense.
  • __gpi - Used by Google to collect user data for ads personalization. Duration: 13 months. Set by: Google AdSense.
  • test_cookie - Used to check if the browser supports cookies. Duration: 15 minutes. Set by: Google DoubleClick.

How to Manage Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these options in your browser's "Preferences," "Settings," or "Privacy" menu. Here are links to cookie management instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of specific cookie-based tracking using these tools:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On
  • Google Ad Personalization: Manage your ad preferences at Google Ads Settings
  • General Ad Opt-Out: Visit Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out or Your Online Choices (EU)

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting. While we respect your privacy preferences, please note that there is currently no industry standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals. Our third-party service providers may or may not honor DNT requests.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, our calculator tools will continue to function normally, as they operate entirely within your browser without relying on cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect:

  • Your cookie consent preference will not be saved, so you may see the consent banner on each visit
  • Advertisements displayed may be less relevant to your interests
  • We will not be able to analyze site usage to improve our tools
