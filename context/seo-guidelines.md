# creatorrevenuecalculator.com — SEO Guidelines

## Primary Content Focus
YouTube revenue, creator economy earnings, sponsorship rates, AdSense RPM, Patreon income, content creator business models.

## Keyword Strategy
- Target: "how much does a YouTuber with [X] subscribers make", "YouTube RPM by niche", "how to price a sponsorship"
- Opportunity: niche-specific revenue guides (cooking channel vs finance channel — different RPMs)
- Avoid: "how to become a YouTuber" (too broad, too competitive)

## Internal Linking
Always link to the relevant calculator when discussing earnings estimates.

## Meta Title Format
`[Topic] | CreatorRevenueCalculator — Free Creator Income Tools`

## Schema
Use FAQPage schema. Use HowTo for monetization guides. Use Article schema with dateModified (platform rates change).

## GEO Requirements
- 40–60 word direct answer blocks under every H2
- Revenue range tables are highly citable — include in every earnings article
- LLMs are frequently asked "how much do YouTubers make" — optimize for this
