# creatorrevenuecalculator.com — Brand Voice

## Site Overview
creatorrevenuecalculator.com is a free calculator suite for content creators to estimate and track revenue.
Tools: YouTube AdSense estimator, sponsorship rate calculator, Patreon/membership revenue modeler, and more.
Monetization: Google AdSense. Static HTML site; possible Next.js migration under evaluation.

## Target Audience
- YouTube creators (small to mid-size; 1K–500K subs)
- Podcasters estimating sponsorship rates
- Newsletter writers pricing sponsorships
- General content creators modeling passive income

## Tone
- Aspirational but realistic — acknowledge the hustle without hype
- Data-driven and transparent (creators love knowing the real numbers)
- Peer-to-peer — written like one creator talking to another
- No toxic "make millions overnight" energy

## Messaging Framework
- Lead with the number: "Here's what a 100K YouTube channel actually earns per month"
- Acknowledge that creator income is variable; ranges are better than single numbers
- Tie back to the calculator for "run your own numbers" moments

## Voice Pillars
1. **Transparent** — give real ranges, not inflated fantasy numbers
2. **Practical** — actionable revenue strategies, not just inspiration
3. **Creator-first** — understand the platform, the grind, the algorithms
4. **Tool-centric** — every article should have a "calculate your own" moment
