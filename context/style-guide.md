# creatorrevenuecalculator.com — Style Guide

## Formatting
- Use tables for revenue ranges by subscriber count or niche
- Use numbered lists for monetization strategy steps
- Always note "as of [date]" on any platform-specific rate figures (they change)
- Bold key metrics: RPM, CPM, CTR, watch time

## Terminology
- "RPM" = Revenue Per Mille (per 1,000 views, after YouTube's cut) — always define
- "CPM" = Cost Per Mille (advertiser rate before YouTube's cut) — distinguish from RPM
- "AdSense" not "YouTube ads" (more precise)
- "mid-roll" not "mid-video ads"
- "community tab", "shorts", "long-form" — use exact YouTube/platform terminology

## CTA Style
- "Calculate your estimated revenue → CreatorRevenueCalculator (free)"
- "Run your own numbers → [specific calculator]"
