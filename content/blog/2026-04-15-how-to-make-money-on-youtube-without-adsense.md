# How to Make Money on YouTube Without Adsense
=====================================================

Are you a YouTube creator who has hit the AdSense limit? Are you having trouble making money from ads? Maybe you are new to YouTube and want to find other ways to earn. There are several ways to make money on YouTube without AdSense. This guide will walk you through your options and show you how much you could earn.

## Understanding the Limitations of AdSense

It helps to know what AdSense requires before looking at other options. YouTube's rules say you need at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months to use AdSense. Even if you qualify, ad earnings can change a lot. CPM rates, which means cost per thousand impressions, often fall between $0.50 and $2.00.

### Knowing Your CPM Rates

It is important to know your CPM rates. YouTube CPM rates change based on your niche, your viewers, and how people interact with ads. For example, gaming creators often earn higher CPM rates than beauty or vlogging creators.

## Alternative Revenue Streams on YouTube

Here are some other ways to make money on your YouTube channel:

### 1. **Sponsorships and Product Placements**

You can partner with brands to promote their products in your videos. This can pay well, but you usually need a solid audience first. Potential earnings: estimated $100-$500 per sponsored video.

#### Tips for Sponsorships and Product Placements:

* Pick brands that fit your niche and values
* Build a good relationship with their marketing teams
* Make content that meets what the brand needs

### 2. **Affiliate Marketing**

You promote a product or service and get a commission when someone buys through your special link. YouTube has millions of users, so affiliate marketing can be a strong option. Potential earnings: estimated 5%-10% of sales made through your affiliate link.

#### Tips for Affiliate Marketing:

* Choose affiliate programs that match your niche and values
* Make content that shows why the product is useful
* Put your affiliate links in video descriptions and cards

### 3. **Merchandise and Virtual Goods Sales**

You can sell items like t-shirts or hats. You can also sell virtual goods like in-game items or exclusive content. You will need a strong online presence to do this well. Potential earnings: estimated $100-$1,000 per month.

#### Tips for Merchandise and Virtual Goods Sales:

* Build a clear brand and a solid product line
* Use social media to promote what you sell
* Give loyal fans exclusive content or rewards

### 4. **YouTube Premium and Super Chat**

YouTube Premium gives viewers an ad-free experience. Super Chat lets fans give larger donations to creators during live streams. Both can add to your income. Potential earnings: estimated $100-$500 per month.

#### Tips for YouTube Premium and Super Chat:

* Build a strong community on YouTube
* Connect with your viewers through Q&A sessions and live streams
* Offer exclusive content for YouTube Premium subscribers

## Navigating YouTube's Monetization Policy

Follow these rules to stay within YouTube's guidelines:

* Always tell your audience when a video is sponsored
* Follow affiliate marketing rules to avoid any problems
* Make sure your merchandise and virtual goods follow YouTube's policies

## Conclusion and Call to Action

These options can help you earn money beyond AdSense. But it is important to be realistic. The numbers above are ranges, not guarantees. Your results depend on things like your niche, how engaged your audience is, and how you market yourself.

To get started, focus on these steps:

* Build a strong presence and community on YouTube
* Make high-quality content that your audience enjoys
* Look into the revenue streams that fit your niche and values
